GENESIS 001

HELLO WORLD

The beginning of the archive.

Mixed Media • 2026

Artist Statement

HELLO WORLD marks the first documented work in Genesis Year One. It represents the beginning of a continuous exploration of line, movement, and form.

What begins as a single uninterrupted gesture becomes a record of intention—an opening signal to a larger body of work that will continue to evolve over time.

Process

This work was created as a timed, continuous drawing process. The accompanying time-lapse documents the physical act of creation from beginning to completion.
