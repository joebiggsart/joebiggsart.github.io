Welcome

For more than two decades I have worked in photography, filmmaking, live events, and visual storytelling. Today those experiences converge into a growing body of original artwork centered on a single continuous line.

Genesis marks the beginning of my public archive. Every artwork, every process video, and every commission from this point forward becomes part of an evolving body of work.
